Homemade Hamburger Helper

Ingredients:

  • Bacon grease
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Ground hamburger meat (1 pound)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nature seasoning
  • Crushed red pepper

  • Bread (for toast)
  • Butter
  • Garlic

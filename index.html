Steak with Fried Potatoes & Onions

Ingredients:

For the Steak:

  • New York strip steak
  • Pink Himalayan salt
  • Cracked black pepper
  • Garlic salt
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic paste

For the Fried Potatoes and Onions:

  • Potato (1 large, peeled and sliced)
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Peanut oil
  • Butter (pats)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
